SMOKE TEST (local, no Telegram)

1) Start server:
   uvicorn app.main:app --reload --port 8080

2) Health:
   curl -s localhost:8080/health

3) Food path:
   curl -s -X POST localhost:8080/route -H 'content-type: application/json' -d '{"user_id":"u1","message":"I want food"}'
   curl -s -X POST localhost:8080/route -H 'content-type: application/json' -d '{"user_id":"u1","message":"1"}'
   curl -s -X POST localhost:8080/route -H 'content-type: application/json' -d '{"user_id":"u1","message":"Get Delivery"}'
   curl -s -X POST localhost:8080/route -H 'content-type: application/json' -d '{"user_id":"u1","message":"later today 7pm"}'

4) Real estate path:
   curl -s -X POST localhost:8080/route -H 'content-type: application/json' -d '{"user_id":"u2","message":"I want to rent a flat for 6 months"}'

5) Telegram (optional):
   Set TELEGRAM_BOT_TOKEN and configure your bot webhook to http(s)://<host>/telegram/webhook
